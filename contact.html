Frequently Asked Questions

What scripts does BharatAkshar support?

We support all major Indian scripts including Devanagari, Bengali, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Gujarati, Gurmukhi, and Odia.

Is BharatAkshar free to use?

Yes, BharatAkshar is completely free and open source.

How accurate is the OCR feature?

Our OCR feature uses Tesseract.js and provides high accuracy for clear text images. Accuracy may vary based on image quality and text complexity.
